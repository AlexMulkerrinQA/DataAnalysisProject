      The [[Academy Award]]s are the oldest awards ceremony for achievements in [[film|motion pictures]]. The '''Academy Award for Best Art Direction''' recognizes achievement in [[art director#Film|art direction]] on a film. The films below are listed with their production year, so the Oscar 2000 for best art direction went to a film from 1999.  In the lists below, the winner of the award for each year is shown first, followed by the other nominees.
